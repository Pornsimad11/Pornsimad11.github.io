Stress Express

“Fun, Stressful and Overwhelming”
Project created by Aim Pornsima Duangratana

Stress Express is a hybrid cards and mobile phones game that pokes fun at stressful, hard-working people. This game will allow players to experience their lives from a macro view where it mimics their busy, overworked and overwhelmed life in a humorous way. The intention of this game is for players to reflect upon the absurdity of a chaotic life, and have them laugh about it.

A game that situates you in a company and makes you compete with other employees to finish the most tasks. Every employee starts with one hand but who knows how many hands and heads you need to win the game.
